About Mars Rovers and Their Cameras

Mars rovers are robotic explorers sent to study and photograph the surface of Mars. They capture high-resolution images and analyze the planet's surface, sending back valuable data to Earth.

Curiosity - Mastcam

The Mastcam is the primary camera on Curiosity, capturing high-resolution images of the Martian surface, including panoramic shots and close-up details of rocks and soil.

Perseverance - Mastcam-Z

The Mastcam-Z camera allows Perseverance to capture high-definition, 3D images of the Martian terrain, enhancing the rover's ability to study the surface in greater detail.

Opportunity - Pancam

The Pancam provides color images of the Martian surface, helping scientists identify terrain features and analyze the composition of rocks and soil.
